Projects

Desgin of an Automated Heating System for Fermentation and Drying of Cacao Beans

The project is focused on the post-harvest process in the cacao industry. The main objective of the project is to create a system that will automate the fermentation and drying process of cacao beans and shortening the total duration of the whole process while maintaining its quality. Several tests were made and it was concluded that the system is accurate, with a total accuracy of 99.34%. Therefore, the shortening of the duration of the whole process while maintaining high-quality fermented cacao beans by the automated heating system is considered successful.

Design of a Smart Alarm Clock Utilizing ESP8266 and Google Firebase

A smart alarm clock that was connected through the cloud using ESP8266 and Google Firebase. The smart alarm's input is controlled by an android application and its output is from the MP3 module that can randomly choose an alarm tone for a every time an alarm goes on, the Liquid crystal display or LCD that generates random mathematical equations that are answerable by the tactile switch and the relay that acts as a switch for the AC outlet, those outputs are accomplished using Arduino software and a Wi-Fi microchip (ESP8266). A different alarm clock was designed for improving the standard alarm clocks with consideration to multiple constraints.

Design of Smart Irrigation System using ESP8266

Commercially available irrigation systems are intended for medium to large scale crop fields and plantations and as such require more resources to operate. Irrigation systems have a common structure design, most have no control to specific conditions leading to unbalanced irrigations and potentially drowning the plants. A control system was designed for an automation of an irrigation system utilizing a device that monitors the soil status and remote access with consideration of multiple constraints. The microcontroller-based control system was designed and yielded an accuracy of 98.18% stopping rate to optimal levels.
